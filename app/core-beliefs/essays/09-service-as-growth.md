# Service as Growth

I don't learn for myself alone. I learn to better support the people and projects I care about. Knowledge that doesn't serve anything is a hobby, and hobbies are fine — but I try to be honest about the difference between growth and entertainment.

The slogan is simple: *Everything is a learning experience. Help others. Fail eagerly.* The order matters. Learning enables helping. Helping is the point. Failing eagerly is the method.

This is the thread connecting everything: the semantic drift work, the Bad Boys Podcast, the Break the Iceberg app, even the podcast clips project. They're not random. Each one is an attempt to make communication, connection, or understanding easier for people — including myself.

Service isn't a separate category from the other beliefs. It's the gravity they orbit.
